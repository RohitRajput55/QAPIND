Our Story

Founded in 2022, QAP IND has grown from a small quality assurance consultancy into a leading provider of comprehensive quality solutions across multiple industries. Our journey began with a simple mission: to help businesses achieve excellence through rigorous quality standards and innovative assurance practices.

Over the years, we've expanded our expertise to cover a wide range of services including quality engineering, ecological solutions, supplier audits, certifications, consulting, and examination services. Our multidisciplinary team of experts brings decades of industry experience to every project we undertake.
